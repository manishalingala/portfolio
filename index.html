About Me

Discover my journey in robotics and AI

I'm currently pursuing my Master's in Electronics and Electrical Engineering at Kyungpook National University, where I am part of the Physical Intelligence Lab. My work lies at the intersection of control systems, robotic manipulation, and machine learning.

From programming drones to follow dynamic trajectories, to training robotic arms to pick up delicate objects, I've always been fascinated by the beautiful blend of logic and creativity that defines engineering.

Beyond the code and circuits, I am a thinker, a learner and a storyteller. I believe every robot I build is a story — of innovation, failure, resilience and breakthrough.

Publications & Research

Contributing to the future of robotics and AI

Vision-Guided Imitation Learning Using Action Chunk Transformers

IEMEK 2024 Conference • Poster Presentation

Adapted ACT algorithm for single-arm robots in vision-based manipulation tasks using CVAE to predict grouped actions from visual and motion data, achieving improved speed, precision, and reliability.

Watch Demo Download PDF

Imitating Precision: Behavioral Cloning for Robot Manipulators

Research Paper • Neural Network Implementation

Developed a neural network system to map end-effector positions to joint angles, achieving high accuracy and smooth trajectory following that outperforms traditional controllers.

Watch Demo Download PDF

QROOT: Integrated Diffusion Transformer for Quadrupedal Locomotion

NeurIPS 2025 • Under Review

Submitted full-length paper combining diffusion transformers with reinforcement learning for advanced quadrupedal robot locomotion control.
